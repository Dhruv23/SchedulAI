Via an initial focus on data storage technologies, it tackles both resilience and efficiency challenges to computing-related problems, and the application of systems-software concepts to broader societal and ethical questions.</span></p><p><span>The Video and Image Processing Laboratory (VIP Lab) investigates state-of-the-art deep learning and signal processing techniques for image and video processing, visual data compression, visual coding for machine intelligence, 3D point cloud coding, and large language-vision models.</span></p><p><span>The Multimedia Visual Processing Laboratory (MVP Lab) supports research in image and video coding (compression and decompression) and processing with visual processing and deep learning methods.</span></p><p><span>The Databases and Information Systems Laboratory (DBIS Lab)  focuses on research in designing, developing, and optimizing data management and information systems. Projects cover areas like database internals (query processing, execution engine, data storage), database architecture (including disaggregated architectures), as well as research in Knowledge Graphs and Semantic Web.</span></p><p><span>The Cloud Laboratory focuses on research in designing, developing, and testing the next generation cloud-based systems and applications: (1) develop and utilize domain-specific hardware for cloud applications, and (2) optimizing existing systems to better serve new and coming applications that rely on the cloud, such as self-driving car, AI/ML/DL/LLM and serverless compute.</span></p><p><span>The Systems &amp; Performance Research Lab conducts research on system software (e.g., runtime systems and compilers) and performance tools (e.g., automatic tuning, profiling, and analysis) to improve the performance of software on parallel and high-performance computer systems. </span></p><p><span>The Internet of Things (IoT) Research Laboratory (SIOTLAB) focuses on designing and developing intelligent sensing solutions, efficient and secure communication platforms and protocols, and seamless edge-to-cloud computing infrastructures that harness artificial intelligence to enhance systems' performance, decision-making, and operational efficiency.</span></p><p><span>The Network Security and Optimization Laboratory focuses on using advanced algorithms and data-driven optimization techniques to solve security related problems in various real-world complex networks. </span></p><p><span>The Trustworthy Computing Laboratory focuses on trust, privacy, and security challenges and solutions for emerging computing systems and networks, such as Agentic-AI systems and their interactions, Distributed Power and Energy Ecosystems, Online social media, Internet-of-Things, and Blockchain.</span></p><p><span>The Data Science Laboratory is devoted to the extraction of knowledge from data and to the theory, design, and implementation of information systems to manage, retrieve, mine, and utilize data.</span></p><p><span>The Responsible AI Laboratory aims to foster interdisciplinary research, promote ethical AI education, and cultivate partnerships that ensure AI advances are safe, equitable, transparent, and beneficial to the broader good of society. </span></p><p><span>The Artificial Intelligence (AI) Laboratory conducts research across diverse facets of AI, including foundational and applied machine learning, and computational creativity, exploring the capabilities of AI systems to be autonomously creative as well as act as co-creative partners. </span></p><p><span>The Machine Learning and Computational Genomics Laboratory focuses on algorithmic design for machine learning problems with real-world applications and impact, especially those with unconventional inputs, such as sparse data, sets of multivariate time series, video streams, and genomics and proteomics data.</span></p><p><span>The Human-Computer Interaction Laboratory (HCI Lab) researches, designs, and develops technologies with social impact, with a focus on AR/VR, social computing, generative AI, and digital wellbeing.</span></p><p><span>The Global Digital Transformation (GDT) Clinic focuses on helping social enterprises, non-profit organizations, and governmental programs that operate in low-resource areas embrace technology. This is done by designing, developing, and deploying customized web/mobile applications that can help them improve and/or scale their operation to better serve their beneficiaries.</span></p><p><span>The Real-Time Autonomous and Intelligent Systems Laboratory (RAISE Lab) focuses on advancing the design of responsive, predictable, and verifiable computing systems for tasks with dynamic timing constraints, particularly in resource-limited environments.</span></p><p><span>The Distributed Data Management and Mining Laboratory (D2M2 Lab) focuses on modern technologies such as blockchain and distributed machine learning for managing and discovering knowledge from distributed data in ubiquitous environments, including edge computing, IoT, social networks, healthcare, and industrial computational systems. The lab aims to design efficient and generalizable approaches with an emphasis on privacy preservation, equity, and sustainability.</span></p><p><span>The Inclusive AI &amp; Multimodal (AIM) Research Laboratory specializes in building and evaluating language-vision tasks, datasets, and models. It focuses on inclusive, responsible, and culturally-aware AI that addresses real-world challenges. Ultimately, the research is for people, and it can enable demographic-aware technologies that make a lasting positive impact on society.</span></p><p><span>The Wireless Intelligent Networks (WIN) Lab focuses on next-generation wireless systems that dynamically adapt, optimize, and make intelligent decisions for efficient resource allocation, multiple access, and network performance. Key focus area spans WiFi 7, 5G/6G protocols, advanced multiple access techniques such as NOMA, multimedia communications, QoE/QoS optimization, network economics, and cognitive radio.</span></p><p><span>For details of faculty research areas, please see</span><span><a href="https://www.scu.edu/engineering/academic-programs/department-of-computer-engineering/"> </a></span><span class=" gduline"><a href="https://www.scu.edu/engineering/academic-programs/department-of-computer-engineering/research/">Research - School of Engineering - Santa Clara University</a></span></p><a id="id.tyjcwt"></a><h2><span class=" gdbold">Lower-Division Courses</span></h2><a id="id.3dy6vkm"></a><h3><span class=" gdbold">10.
